Why Little Champs ?

Little Champs is a village school which is at the centre of the local community. We aim to enrich all our children's learning with the many benefits, this offers. There are strong parent links which foster a secure environment for the children to embark on their education.

We hope to equip the children with both the academic tools for economic success and the social and emotional skills for happy lives. We want every child to feel empowered to ask questions, take risks, make mistakes and test ideas and yes, we do let the children climb ladder of Success!

Please come and visit us to see for yourself !
